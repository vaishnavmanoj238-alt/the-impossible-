What This Is

The Impossible is a public record of serious attempts at difficult things. It exists to test limits that are usually accepted too early. This project focuses on effort, time, and honest documentation -- not motivation or promises.

The Method

  1. An expectation exists
  2. A limit is assumed
  3. A serious attempt is made
  4. Effort is sustained for a fixed time
  5. The result is recorded honestly

Decisions are made only after the attempt ends.
